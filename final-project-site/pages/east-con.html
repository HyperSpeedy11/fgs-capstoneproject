EAST Conference

EAST conference is a yearly event that brings almost all of the best EAST members to Hot Springs, Arkansas to display the best projects in their school. This event usually happens around March.

SCHS EAST Conference 2016 Members

Every year, several students from the high school and middle school are chosen to go to the annual EAST Conference. These are the students that usually work the hardest and have the best projects in EAST. These students are very sophisticated, collaborate with other people to make their project help the community, and very good working with other teams/groups.
