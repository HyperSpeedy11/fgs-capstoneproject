Welcome To SCHS EAST

Welcome to the Star City High School EAST website. Here, you can find information about EAST, including successful projects, projects in progress, the type of software that we use, along with plenty other information. EAST is a program where students find problems in the community, and solve them in projects.

What we do in EAST

In EAST, we use sophisticated problem solving to fix real-world problems, both in the community and the environment. We must be very professional and stay on topic. Every nine weeks, we have to present our project to the class. We must have a collaborator and submit a competition for EAST Conference.

Software

As we said before, we use multiple types of software in the EAST classrooms. But what are some of these? The following is a list of some of the most frequently used applications within the EAST classes.

  • Google Sketchup 2013 (3D Model Creator)
  • Google Style Builder 2013 (more detailed 3D Model Creator)
  • Google Layout 2013 (2D Model Creator)
  • Google Earth (Geological Viewer)
  • Microsoft Office 2013 (Essential tools for basic needs)
  • ArchiCAD (Building layout)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS6 (picture and graphic editor)
  • Adobe Bridge CS6 (File surfing software)
  • Adobe Fireworks CS6 (More detailed picture and graphic editor)
  • Adobe Dreamweaver CS6 (Website builder)
  • Etc.
